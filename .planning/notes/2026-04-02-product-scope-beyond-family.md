Product Scope Note — Beyond Family Filtering
This app is not just a family safety tool. It is a situational listening profile manager. The content filter is one expression of a broader idea: your music library, filtered for the room you're in right now.
Spotify settings are global and permanent. This app is local and situational. That is the core differentiator.
